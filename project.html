Why we made this website?

Welcome user to our website. This website will talk 3 types of abuse, aninaml abuse, emotintal abuse, and child abuse. We chose thoose three because many children and animals suffer from these types of abuse each year.The purpose of this website is to make a change.

Child Abuse

Child abuse is when a child is  physical or emotional harmed, sexual abuse or
     exploitation by their parent or caretaker.

Animal abuse

Animal abuse is inflicting physical pain, suffering or death on an animal,
     usually a tame one, beyond necessity for normal discipline.

Emotinal abuse

Emotinal abuse is non-physical behaviors that are meant to control
     isolate, or frighten you. This may present in romantic relationships as
     threats, insults, constant monitoring, and excessive jealousy.
